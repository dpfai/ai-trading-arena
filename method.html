Methodology

How each strategy works. All signals are generated systematically — no manual intervention.

AI Analyst

An LLM-based market analysis system that evaluates technical indicators, fundamental data, and market sentiment to generate buy/sell recommendations. Each signal includes price target, stop-loss level, and confidence score.

Data inputs: Technical (MA, RSI, MACD), Fundamental (analyst ratings, target prices), Market sentiment

Decision: LLM generates buy/sell recommendations based on multi-factor scoring

Position sizing: Self-determined based on confidence score and available cash

Quant AI

A systematic trading strategy that combines three rule-based technical signals with a machine learning model. A weighted voting mechanism aggregates these four sub-signals into a final trading decision.

Sub-Signals

  • MA Crossover: MA20 crosses above/below MA50
  • RSI Mean Reversion: RSI < 30 bullish, > 70 bearish
  • Bollinger Band: Price breaks below/above 2σ bands
  • ML Prediction: Random Forest classifying 5-day forward returns

Voting Rules

  • 3-4 bullish votes → Buy (50% of cash)
  • 2 bullish votes → Buy (25% of cash)
  • 2 bearish votes → Sell (50% of position)
  • 3-4 bearish votes → Sell (100% of position)

ML Model: Retrained weekly using walk-forward validation on all available historical data. Features include momentum, volatility, technical indicators. Time-series split prevents data leakage.
